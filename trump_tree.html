The Trump pedigree tree

Press a name on the pedigree tree and an image of the person will be shown.
For grand children of Donald Trump a family portrait will be shown.
You can move the family tree around by clicking and holding on the tree or the white space around it.

Here an attribution to the author of a a portrait will be shown.

Motivation for choices in visualization

As a pedigree tree is all about children, the couplings that have made the most children har foremost and leftmost.
And as this tree is all about Donald Trump, he is of course at the top.
The Trump family is a very traditional and conservative family, therefore each person is sexed with conservative colors:
Pink for women and blue for men.
As the family tree is very wide, Donald Trump having many marriages, you cannot see the whole tree all the time,
but we have made it possible to move the tree around.
When you click on a node of a person in the tree, a picture of the person is shown at the bottom of the page.
The picture could've also been shown on the node itself, in the tree.
Below the picture, a notation of the author of the picture is displayed.

Possible improvements to the visualization

The visualitation could be improved by more interactivity and more information about the people and their relations.
An animation showing the relations between people changing over time would make for a great insight into the
relations of Donald Trump. This could show a counter of what the current year was, and then new links of relations
between people could pop-up, along with new people popping up in the diagram as babies were born.
More information about people could be shown, when they are clicked upon. This could be a summary of a Wikipedia-article,
the latest tweets or maybe a link to a photo series.
The story itself could easily be guided with what information is shown. A negative story could show what a person would
deem as unintelligent tweets by Donald Trump, critisize his policies and sexual harassment allegations
A more positive story, supporting Donald Trump, could show his patriotic tweets, show his big assemblies of people and
show what that person would deem as positive policies.
